Actor Tips:

  1. Don't smile or laugh (unless the scene calls for it)
  2. Don't look at the camera
  3. Avoid the temptation to add your own sound effects (everything will be added later)
  4. Show emotion.

Props for Actors to bring if possible:

  1. Ski jacket (spacesuit)
  2. White T-shirt
  3. extra change of cloths. Something plain colored that would not be out of place on a spaceship in the year 2050.

Screen tests:

  1. Rent Alien movie.
  2. Alien face-sucker pops out toward camera.
  3. Film from inside car (escape shuttle) while backing out of garage.
  4. Kitchen - alien falls on Ripley
