Math League team scores

Archived mid-year scores from the Pioneer Math League (December 2009). This is a historical snapshot, not a current season page.

These are the team scores at the midyear point. There were 6 teams with 5 or six students on each team. They got points by answering questions, and by playing the team competition game. They could occasionally lose points for talking out of turn.
