Test 3 Analysis
This test was all about percents. Yes the problems were hard, but we have been working with percents for a few weeks now. The maximum score was 80%, the minimum was 0.8%. The mean score was 24%, and the median score was 18%. This tells you that the distribution was skewed to the lower end. The highest 3 scores were 80%, 56% and 56%. Since the scores were not as high as I was hoping for it is likely that the students may see another similar test in their future.
