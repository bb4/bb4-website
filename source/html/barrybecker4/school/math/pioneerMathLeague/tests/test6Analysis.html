Test 6 Analysis (first probability test)
  The kids did very well on the probability test today. The average score was 53%. Even more encouraging was the fact that the minimum score was 30%. The high score was 85%. This is much higher than on the first percent test, but then again maybe the questions were a little easier.

There were a couple of students that left the first question blank, but got many of the other harder problems correct. There were also a few that did not put 0 for number 9, even though I thought that was pretty much a free point. In all, though, they did quite well for the amount of discussion we have had so far.

  Next week we will play the three card thriller and Monte Hall games to help them see that probabilites can be counterintuitive in some real word situations.

There will be another probability test is a couple of weeks that will be a little more challenging.
