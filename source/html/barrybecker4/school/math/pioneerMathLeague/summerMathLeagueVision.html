Vision
I would like to hold Math League meetings every Friday at my house through the summer months. The format will be similar in some ways to the current math league sessions, but there will be some differences.

1) The first 45 minutes or so will be similar the weekly sessions during the school year was. We will have teams and discuss topics, and have occasional tests that I prepare.

2) The time after that will consist mostly of free play in which we engage in math oriented games (such as Monopoly, MindTrap, Risk, Go, Chess, Poker, Paraos, Blockade, and others) as well as other fun activities. Parents can pick up their children any time during this second hour or let them stay the full time.

3) I invite the parents to get involved by actively participating or through a yahoo group (email list). I was not allowed to have an email group while associated with Pioneer, but hopefully after graduation, it will not be an issue.

4) I will have to charge some nominal fee to cover copies, materials, food, and my time.  Whatever dues I ultimately decide on, I'm sure it will be much less than similar alternatives.
