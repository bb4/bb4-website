5th Grade GATE math tests and answers (2009–2010)

Note:

Though these tests were given to 5th graders, they are all good problems for middle schoolers. Trying these problems will be good preparation for AMS math league for the 2011/12 school year. I will replace this list with current year tests after they are given and graded.

Here are the tests and answers for those tests that have been administered so far this year. These tests are provided here for the benefit of the students and their parents.

  • 11/13/2009 — Test 3 (Percents)

  • 1/15/2009 — Test 5 (Percents reloaded)
  • 1/29/2009 — Test 6 (Probability)
  • 1/29/2010 — Test 7 (Probability again)
  • 3/12/2010 — Test 8 (Unit cancelation)
