🎲 Probability Quiz

1. What is probability?



Probability tells us how likely an event is to happen.

2. Probability of flipping heads?



A fair coin has 2 equal outcomes, so probability is 1/2.

3. Probability of rolling a 3?



There are 6 outcomes and only one is a 3 → 1/6.

4. Which probability is impossible?



A probability of 0 means it cannot happen.

5. Which probability is certain?



A probability of 1 means it will definitely happen.

6. Probability of picking red (3 red, 2 blue)?



Total = 5 marbles, 3 are red → 3/5.

7. Spinner with 4 equal colors: probability of green?



Each of 4 equal sections → 1 out of 4.

8. Probability of picking a heart?



13 hearts out of 52 cards → 1/4.

9. Probability of chocolate (6 out of 10)?



6/10 simplifies to 3/5.

10. Probability of an even number?



Even numbers (2,4,6) → 3 out of 6 = 1/2.
